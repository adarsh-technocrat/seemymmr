Filter your analytics data to focus on what matters most. PostMetric provides powerful filtering options to help you understand your traffic.

## Date range filters

Select a date range to analyze:

- **Today** - View today's data
- **Last 7 days** - View the past week
- **Last 30 days** - View the past month
- **Custom range** - Select any date range
- **All time** - View all historical data

## Source filters

Filter by traffic source:

- **Direct** - Direct visits (no referrer)
- **Search** - Search engine traffic
- **Social** - Social media traffic
- **Referral** - Referral traffic from other websites
- **Email** - Email campaign traffic
- **Paid** - Paid advertising traffic

## Location filters

Filter by geographic location:

- **Country** - Filter by country
- **Region** - Filter by region/state
- **City** - Filter by city

## Device filters

Filter by device type:

- **Desktop** - Desktop computers
- **Mobile** - Mobile devices
- **Tablet** - Tablets

## Browser and OS filters

Filter by:

- **Browser** - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.
- **Operating System** - Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android

## Path filters

Filter by page path:

- **Specific path** - View data for a specific page
- **Path pattern** - Use patterns to match multiple pages

## Combining filters

You can combine multiple filters to get precise insights:

- Traffic from Google in the US on mobile devices
- Revenue from email campaigns in the last 30 days
- Conversions from social media on desktop

## Exclude filters

Exclude specific data from your analytics:

- **Exclude your IP** - Don't count your own visits
- **Exclude countries** - Exclude specific countries
- **Exclude paths** - Exclude specific pages (e.g., admin pages)
- **Exclude hostnames** - Exclude specific subdomains

See [Exclude visits](/advanced/exclude-visits) for more details.

## Save filters

Save frequently used filter combinations for quick access.

## Export filtered data

Export your filtered data to CSV for further analysis.
